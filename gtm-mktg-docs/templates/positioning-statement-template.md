# Positioning Statement Template

## Template

**For [target audience], [product name] is [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [alternatives], [product name] [differentiator].**

## Components

### Target Audience
[Who is this product for? Be specific about the user type, role, or segment.]

### Category
[What type of product is this? How would you describe it in one phrase?]

### Key Benefit
[What is the primary value or benefit this product delivers? Focus on outcomes, not features.]

### Alternatives
[What do people currently use instead? This could be competitors, substitutes, or "doing nothing".]

### Differentiator
[What makes this product unique or better than alternatives? Be specific and credible.]

## Example

**For solo founders building their first product, TaskTracker is a project management tool that helps them stay organized without complexity. Unlike Trello or Asana, TaskTracker focuses on simplicity and speed, letting founders manage tasks in minutes, not hours.**

**Components:**
- **Target Audience**: Solo founders building their first product
- **Category**: Project management tool
- **Key Benefit**: Stay organized without complexity
- **Alternatives**: Trello or Asana
- **Differentiator**: Focuses on simplicity and speed, letting founders manage tasks in minutes, not hours

## Usage Tips

- Keep it to one sentence if possible
- Be specific about your audience
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Make your differentiator credible and testable
- Test your positioning with target users before finalizing
