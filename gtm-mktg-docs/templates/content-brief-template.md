## Example

**Content:** "How Solo Founders Can Stay Organized Without Complexity"  
**Content Type:** Blog post

**Content Goal:** Drive awareness and signups for TaskTracker

**Target Audience:** Solo founders building their first product

**Key Message:** Solo founders don't need complex project management tools; they need simplicity and speed.

**Supporting Points:**
- Most project management tools are built for teams, not individuals
- Solo founders waste time on features they don't need
- Simple tools help founders focus on building, not managing

**Call to Action:** Sign up for TaskTracker (free trial)

**Structure:**
1. Introduction: The problem solo founders face
2. Why existing tools are too complex
3. What solo founders actually need
4. How TaskTracker solves this
5. Call to action

**Tone:** Friendly, practical, understanding of solo founder challenges

**Primary Channel:** Company blog

**Success Metrics:**
- Page views: 500 in first week
- Signups from content: 20
- Engagement: 5% click-through to signup
